# SloppyQuizz

SloppyQuizz is an app for creating, playing, and sharing quizzes with other users.

The goal is simple: let anyone create their own quizzes, launch them in a live room, and invite other players to join in real time.

## What the app does

- Create custom quizzes with your own questions.
- Edit each quiz question like a canvas, with images, GIFs powered by GIPHY, blind tests, and video clips.
- Share quizzes with other players.
- Join a room to take part in a live game.
- Play together in real time.
- Follow scores during the game.
- Compare results through a global leaderboard.
